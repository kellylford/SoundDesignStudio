# Preset Library Reference

Complete reference for all presets in Sound Design Studio.

## How to Use Presets

### Method 1: Preview Presets Dialog
1. **Press Alt+R** or open **Presets ‚Üí Preview Presets**
2. Use arrow keys to navigate (auto-plays each preset)
3. Click **"Use Preset"** to add layers to your document

### Method 2: Direct Add from Menu
1. **Press Alt+R** or open **Presets** menu
2. Navigate categories and click a preset name
3. Layers are immediately added to your document

**Important**: Presets **ADD** layers to your document, they don't replace existing ones!

## Musical Instruments

### Piano & Keys
- **Electric Piano**: Warm piano with brightness (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Fundamental: C4 (261.63 Hz) sine wave with medium sustain
  - Brightness: C5 (523.26 Hz) triangle wave for sparkle
  
- **Bell**: Strike + shimmer with harmonics (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Strike: 440 Hz sine with long decay
  - Shimmer: 880 Hz sine with harmonics (octave, fifth enabled)
  
- **Organ**: Base + octave + fifth (3 layers, simultaneous)
  - Base: A3 (220 Hz) sustained tone
  - Octave: A4 (440 Hz) for richness
  - Fifth: E4 (330 Hz) for harmonic depth

### Strings
- **Violin**: Bow + air texture (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Bow: 440 Hz sawtooth with slow attack
  - Air: 880 Hz triangle for breathiness
  
- **Cello**: Low string + resonance (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Low String: G3 (196 Hz) sawtooth with rich sustain
  - Resonance: G2 (98 Hz) sine for depth

### Drums & Percussion
- **Kick Drum**: Thump + click (2 layers, sequential)
  - Thump: 60 Hz sine, quick decay
  - Click: 2000 Hz sawtooth, very short
  
- **Snare Hit**: Body + snap (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Body: 200 Hz sine with medium attack
  - Snap: 4000 Hz sawtooth, sharp attack
  
- **Hi-Hat**: High + mid frequencies (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - High: 8000 Hz sawtooth, very short
  - Mid: 4000 Hz square for body

## Sound Effects

### Impacts
- **Heavy Impact**: Boom + crack (2 layers, sequential)
  - Boom: 80 Hz sine with sub-bass
  - Crack: 2000 Hz sawtooth burst
  
- **Metal Clang**: Strike + ring with harmonics (2 layers, sequential)
  - Strike: 400 Hz square with sharp attack
  - Ring: 800 Hz sine with harmonics, long release

### Transitions
- **Whoosh**: Air + wind (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Air: 600 Hz triangle, medium attack
  - Wind: 300 Hz sine, slower fade
  
- **Sweep Up**: Low ‚Üí mid ‚Üí high (3 layers, sequential)
  - Low: 100 Hz triangle
  - Mid: 300 Hz triangle  
  - High: 900 Hz triangle
  
- **Sweep Down**: High ‚Üí mid ‚Üí low (3 layers, sequential)
  - High: 900 Hz triangle
  - Mid: 300 Hz triangle
  - Low: 100 Hz triangle

### Atmosphere
- **Rumble**: Sub bass + texture (2 layers, simultaneous)
  - Sub Bass: 50 Hz sine with slow attack
  - Texture: 100 Hz triangle with harmonics
  
- **Drone**: Base + overtone + whisper (3 layers, simultaneous)
  - Base: 110 Hz sine, sustained
  - Overtone: 220 Hz sine, quieter
  - Whisper: 330 Hz triangle, subtle

## Usage Tips

### Playback Modes
- **Sequential**: Layers play one after another (build dramatic sounds)
- **Simultaneous**: All layers play together (rich, complex tones)

### Customization
1. Add a preset to your document
2. Select a layer (Left/Right arrows)
3. Press **Enter** to open designer
4. Modify parameters to taste
5. **Ctrl+S** to save your custom sound document

### Layer Counts
- **1-Layer Presets**: Simple, clear sounds (Button Hover, Message Notification)
- **2-Layer Presets**: Most common, versatile (Electric Piano, Heavy Impact)
- **3-Layer Presets**: Complex, rich sounds (Organ, Power-Up Collect)

## Sound Design Lessons from Presets

**Bell**: High frequency + harmonics = shimmer
**Kick Drum**: Low freq first, high freq for attack
**Organ**: Harmonically related frequencies
**Sweep Up**: Rising frequencies = upward motion
**Drone**: Long attack/sustain = atmosphere
**Power Up**: Sequential = sense of progression
**Alien Growl**: Multiple octaves = richness
**Laser**: High freq + sawtooth = sci-fi
