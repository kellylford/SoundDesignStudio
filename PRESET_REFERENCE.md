# Preset Quick Reference

## How to Use Presets

1. **Menu ‚Üí Presets** ‚Üí Choose category ‚Üí Choose subcategory ‚Üí Click preset
2. Press **Alt+P** to play
3. Press **Enter** on a layer to customize it
4. Press **Ctrl+S** to save your customized version

---

## Musical Instruments

### Piano & Keys
- **Electric Piano**: Warm piano with brightness (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Bell**: Strike + shimmer with harmonics (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Organ**: Base + octave + fifth (3 layers, simultaneous)

### Strings
- **Violin**: Bow + air texture (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Cello**: Low string + resonance (2 layers, simultaneous)

### Drums & Percussion
- **Kick Drum**: Thump + click (2 layers, sequential)
- **Snare Hit**: Body + snap (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Hi-Hat**: High + mid frequencies (2 layers, simultaneous)

---

## Sound Effects

### Impacts
- **Heavy Impact**: Boom + crack (2 layers, sequential)
- **Metal Clang**: Strike + ring with harmonics (2 layers, sequential)

### Transitions
- **Whoosh**: Air + wind (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Sweep Up**: Low ‚Üí mid ‚Üí high (3 layers, sequential)

### Atmosphere
- **Rumble**: Sub bass + texture (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Drone**: Base + overtone + whisper (3 layers, simultaneous)

---

## User Interface

### Clicks
- **Button Click**: Click + tail (2 layers, sequential)
- **Toggle On**: Low ‚Üí high (2 layers, sequential)
- **Toggle Off**: High ‚Üí low (2 layers, sequential)

### Notifications
- **Success**: Ascending dings (2 layers, sequential)
- **Error**: Buzz + harsh (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Message**: Two-tone beep (2 layers, sequential)

---

## Game Sounds

### Weapons
- **Laser Gun**: Beam + zap (2 layers, simultaneous)
- **Sword Swing**: Whoosh + whistle (2 layers, sequential)

### Pickups
- **Coin**: Double pling (2 layers, sequential)
- **Power Up**: Three-stage rise (3 layers, sequential)

### Enemies
- **Alien Growl**: Low + growl + texture (3 layers, simultaneous)
- **Robot Beep**: Triple beep pattern (3 layers, sequential)

## Playback Modes

**Sequential**: Layers play one after another (like a sequence)
**Simultaneous**: All layers play together (mixed)

---

## By Number of Layers

**2-Layer Presets**: Most common, good for learning
- Electric Piano, Bell, Violin, Cello
- Heavy Impact, Whoosh, Laser Gun
- Button Click, Success, Message

**3-Layer Presets**: Complex, rich sounds
- Organ, Sweep Up, Drone
- Power Up, Alien Growl, Robot Beep

---

## By Playback Mode

### Simultaneous (Mixed Together)
- Electric Piano, Bell, Organ
- Violin, Cello
- Snare Hit, Hi-Hat
- Metal Clang (after strike)
- Whoosh, Rumble, Drone
- Error
- Laser Gun
- Alien Growl

### Sequential (One After Another)
- Kick Drum
- Heavy Impact, Metal Clang
- Sweep Up
- Button Click, Toggle On, Toggle Off
- Success, Message
- Sword Swing
- Coin, Power Up
- Robot Beep

## Sound Design Lessons from Presets

**Bell**: High frequency + harmonics = shimmer
**Kick Drum**: Low freq first, high freq for attack
**Organ**: Harmonically related frequencies
**Sweep Up**: Rising frequencies = upward motion
**Drone**: Long attack/sustain = atmosphere
**Power Up**: Sequential = sense of progression
**Alien Growl**: Multiple octaves = richness
**Laser**: High freq + sawtooth = sci-fi

## Customization Ideas

1. Load "Bell" ‚Üí Change frequencies for different pitches
2. Load "Kick Drum" ‚Üí Adjust attack for punchier sound
3. Load "Sweep Up" ‚Üí Add more layers for longer sweep
4. Load "Drone" ‚Üí Change waveforms for different textures
5. Load any preset ‚Üí Add your own layer!
